Pizza

Description

This is a great recipe when you don't want to wait for the dough to rise. You just mix it and allow it to rest for 5 minutes and then it's ready to go! It yields a soft, chewy crust.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven
  2. Place water in a warm bowl; add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt; beat till smooth
  4. Rest for 5 minutes
  5. Transfer to prepared pizza pan
  6. Spread crust and toppings of choice
  7. Bake till golden brown
